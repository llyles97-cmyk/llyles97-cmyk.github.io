About

Lyles Mom

I'm a personalization strategist and audience intelligence analyst based in Philadelphia. For the past three years I've been building data-driven engagement systems at a global pharmaceutical company — owning personalization strategy across 10+ international markets, architecting audience segmentation models, and translating behavioral data into decisions that actually get implemented.

My work lives at a specific intersection: where measurement systems fail the behavior they were built to capture. Chart position isn't the audience. Watch time isn't the subscriber. Outreach sent isn't conversion achieved. I build frameworks that make those gaps visible — and analytics that price them.

Outside of that, I'm a DJ (DJ Ang3L), a poet working on a collection called The Furthest Wave, and the person behind Cult Circulation — a media brand analyzing how attention and trends move through culture. I go to the movies most weekends and have strong opinions about what constitutes a real summer roll.

"The gap between what institutions measure and what audiences actually do is not a bug — it is a feature of how measurement systems get built."

I'm currently targeting roles in audience intelligence, consumer insights, and measurement strategy at music, media, healthcare, and corporate analytics organizations. If the role involves finding the signal inside noisy data and translating it into something a stakeholder can act on, I want to hear about it.
